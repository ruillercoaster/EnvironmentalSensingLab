<br/>
Multi-scale studies on the seasonality of SIF, hyperspectral reflectance, canopy color, and GPP in Niwot Ridge, Colorado. 
          
These papers feature a canopy-level telescope, PhotoSpec (a.k.a. BEYONCE).
